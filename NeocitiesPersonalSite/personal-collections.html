(Definitely explains the amount of "red screen of death" I was subject to!) So I'm trying to build that original collection again!

{PS1 games here}

I collect PS1 games too! This stuff was before my time (technically a bunch of PS2 stuff was also before my time but whatever), but with PS1 games being easily playable on PS2, and me having a PS2, it meant it made a lot of sense to collect! Unlike collecting for its rival - the N64 seems super cool but the only other way to play its games is emulation, and as much as I'd love to see that goofy ahh controller in person one day, it didn't make sense to go buy an entire console for 300 possible games (and the portion of those that are actually good). I do have Kirby 64 though, for my mission to collect all Kirby games!

The Botanicals have honestly become some of my favourite sets, they're pretty affordable compared to other sets Lego puts out now, and they're imo the easiest of all sets to display, because you can just pop them on any of the tabletops around your house for some funky decor, and you won't look like a self-unaware manchild like you would with any other sets. (Jabbing at myself there just to clarify!) You can make many other sets marketed for adults work for display, but a lot more intentionality has to go into that and I'm lazy lol.

Also, Lego does plants of all kinds, but I feel the style lends itself to succulents in particular, which are my favourite kind of plant. (Cacti, my beloved) :3
